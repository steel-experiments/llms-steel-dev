The Session Viewer is the live viewing surface for a Steel session. It lets operators see what the browser is doing while the run is still in progress.

Teams use it for debugging, approvals, and controlled human intervention.

## Why it matters

- It gives a real-time operator surface for browser runs.
- It supports human-in-the-loop workflows without abandoning the session.
- It complements Replay, which is the after-the-fact record.
