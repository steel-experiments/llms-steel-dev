Steel Cloud is the managed version of Steel. It hosts browser sessions for you and adds the surrounding infrastructure needed for production runs, including observability, file handling, and other managed workflow surfaces.

It is the default choice when you need concurrency, managed anti-bot surfaces, or shared operator tooling.

## Why it matters

- It removes most browser fleet operations from your team.
- It provides the hosted environment behind many Steel integrations.
- It is the natural step up from local or self-hosted workflows.
