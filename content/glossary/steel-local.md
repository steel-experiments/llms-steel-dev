Steel Local is the version of Steel you run yourself, typically for local development, internal environments, or workloads that need tighter infrastructure control.

It follows the same core session model but does not provide every managed surface that Steel Cloud does.

## Why it matters

- It gives teams a lower-level deployment option.
- It works well for development and controlled workloads.
- It is the trade-off point between autonomy and managed infrastructure.
