CAPTCHA solving covers the mechanisms used to complete or route interactive verification challenges that block automation.

For production agent systems, CAPTCHA solving is usually part of a broader anti-bot and approval strategy rather than a magic bypass.

## Why it matters

- CAPTCHA events break unattended workflows.
- Managed solving reduces bespoke integrations and retries.
- It should be paired with clear policy on where automated solving is acceptable.
